### Bug Report: Incorrect Product Display in Hair Category - Shampoo

**Bug ID:** BR-001  
**Reported By:** Ana Carvalho    
**Severity:** Medium  
**Priority:** High  

**Environment:**  
- Website: https://www.oboticario.pt/  
- Browser: Chrome (latest)  
- Device: Desktop  

**Description:**  
When navigating to the "Cabelo" category and the "Shampoo" subcategory, some products on the second page of results do not match the selected subcategory. Specifically, a product from the "Conditioner" subcategory is displayed.

**Steps to Reproduce:**  
1. Click on the menu icon  
2. Select the "Products" tab  
3. Select the category "Cabelo"  
4. Choose the subcategory "Shampoo"  
5. Verify the products on the first page (all correct)  
6. Click on "2" to go to the second page  
7. Observe that a product from "Condicionador" appears

**Expected Result:**  
- All products displayed in the "Shampoo" subcategory should belong to that subcategory, including all pages of results.

**Actual Result:**  
- On the first page, all products were correct.  
- On the second page, one product from the "Condicionador" subcategory appeared, which does not match the applied "Shampoo" filter
